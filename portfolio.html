Omotrash

This app streamlines recycling by allowing users to log in securely with their Google accounts via Firebase. Users can submit details about recyclable waste, including its nature and pickup address. Integrated with Google Maps, the app makes it easy to locate waste pickup points. The displayed data includes a button guiding waste collectors to the pickup location using Google Maps, promoting efficient and sustainable recycling.
