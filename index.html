Achievements

Memulai Pemrograman dengan Kotlin (Dicoding Indonesia)

I received this certificate upon completing the IDCamp 2023 Android Developer path. IDCamp is a scholarship program organized by Indosat Ooredoo Hutchison, and the classes are hosted on Dicoding.

Gold Medal PKMM AST-PTMA

I received this certificate after winning a gold medal in the PKMM (Program Kreativitas Mahasiswa Perguruan Tinggi Muhammadiyah 'Aisyiyah) competition. I achieved the Gold medal as part of a team with our project titled 'Trashify: Ekosistem Masyarakat Sadar Pemilahan Sampah Menggunakan Aplikasi Berbasi Android Dengan Menggunakan Algoritma Machine Learning.' The Program Kreativitas Mahasiswa Perguruan Tinggi Muhammadiyah 'Aisyiyah (PKMM) is an initiative by the Association of Science and Technology of Muhammadiyah 'Aisyiyah Universities (AST-PTMA). It provides a platform for students to showcase their creativity and innovation. The program has various categories, such as PKM Riset Eksakta (Research in Exact Sciences), PKM Kewirausahaan (Entrepreneurship), PKM Pengabdian Kepada Masyarakat (Community Service), PKM Penerapan IPTEK (Application of Science and Technology), and PKM Karsa Cipta (Innovative Idea). I took part in the Karsa Cipta category, specifically focusing on Innovative Ideas, with two separate teams, and I was a member of one of those teams.

Bronze Medal PKMM AST-PTMA

I received this certificate after winning a bronze medal in the PKMM (Program Kreativitas Mahasiswa Perguruan Tinggi Muhammadiyah 'Aisyiyah) competition. I achieved the bronze medal as part of a team with our project titled 'Monitoring Dan Pencatatan Konsultasi Dokter Dan Pasien Berbasis Speech Recognition Untuk Mencegah Terjadinya Malapraktik.'

Participants of INVFEST 7.0 Competition

I received this certificate for participating in a software development competition organized by Telkom University Purwokerto, called INVFEST 7.0, in 2022. This was my team's first competition, but unfortunately, we didn't win. So, this serves as a participant certificate. While we didn't secure a victory in our first competition, the experience was valuable, and we learned a lot from the challenges faced during the event.
